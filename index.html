Tapenade (Olive Spread)


This is a really simple dish to prepare and itâ€™s always a big hit at parties.

Ingredients

  1. 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes

  2. 2 large garlic cloves

  3. 2/3 c. kalamata olive

  4. 1 t. capers

Instructions

  • Combine tomatoes and garlic in

  • Blend until as smooth as possibe.

  • Add capers and olives.

  • Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.



Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm.
